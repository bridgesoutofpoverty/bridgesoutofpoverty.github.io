OUR PARTNERS




COLLABORATION

COLLABORATION IS ONE OF OUR CORE VALUES.

Approximately 75 key organizations provide services to people living in poverty in our community.


We have forde the Omaha Poverty Collaborative and are inviting all these organizations to work together in order to:

  • Use the available dollars most effectively
  • Let everyone do what they do best
  • Plan together
  • Deliver services and programs together
  • Share resources where appropriate
  • Share information that is mutually beneficial

Getting Ahead participants may receive training through Omaha Bridges Out of Poverty. OR through the organization they currently work with.
OR both!

SERVICES AVAILABLE TO OUR PARTNERS

Our partner charitable service organizations face the challenges that every business faces today and in many cases do not have the dollars available for high level business consulting services.

However, there is a tremendous resource in our retired professionals in the community and other willing volunteers.

We have created a catalogue of professionals that are available with a variety of organization skills, business skills, etc. and these individuals are available to consult with our partners on a volunteer basis (you may need to provide coffee).

AN ADDED VALUE.

These volunteer consultants are being trained in Bridges Out of Poverty concepts so that they better understand the people you serve.



ONE MORE BENEFIT.

These volunteer consultants meet on a regular basis to share ideans, solutions and resources which provides additional value to our partners.
